ABOUT MOUNIR

Introducing Mounir Lababidi, the talented Syrian pianist and keyboard player, born on June 1, 1955, in Damascus. Mounir's musical adventure began in 1977 when he joined the band "Fire Ball." Despite being self-taught, Mounir's dedication to his craft led to continuous improvement over the years. Throughout his career, Mounir joined famous singers on their tours like “Asala Nasri” , adding his magical touch to their performances. Beyond the stage, he shared his expertise by giving piano lessons, inspiring numerous children to embark on their own musical journeys. Additionally, Mounir's creativity extended to the realm of television, where he served as a studio supervisor, composing music for beloved children's TV series. His compositions captivated young hearts and minds, leaving an indelible mark on generations. Today, Mounir Lababidi continues to inspire with his passion for music and unwavering commitment to artistic excellence. Join him as he weaves melodies that resonate with the soul, leaving an enduring legacy of musical brilliance and inspiration.

FIRE BALL Band

Piano Keyboard Player

From their humble beginnings in 1977 until 2001, Mounir worked on a musical adventure that would captivate audiences across Syria. Night after night, the Fire Ball band brought the magic of music to life in some of Syria's most prestigious restaurants. Their daily performances became a staple of the dining experience, enchanting patrons with soulful melodies and electrifying rhythms. With Mounir's skillful piano and keyboard playing, the Fire Ball band created an atmosphere of joy and celebration, transforming ordinary evenings into unforgettable experiences. Their seamless harmonies and infectious energy drew crowds from far and wide, earning them a reputation as one of Syria's premier musical acts.
