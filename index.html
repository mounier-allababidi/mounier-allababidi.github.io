About me

I'm a skilled pianist and keyboard player from Syria, born on June 1, 1955, in Damascus. My musical journey took flight in 1977 when I became a part of the band "Fire Ball." Alongside my performances with Fire Ball, I've had the honor of accompanying famous singers like Asala Nasri on their tours, contributing my musical flair to their shows. Beyond the stage, I've also provided piano lessons to young musicians. I also served as a studio supervisor, converted English children's TV series to Arabic version . Today, my journey in music continues as I keep on discovering new techniques on my piano on a daily basis.
